Who I am:

I live there for 5 years as of now. since a young age I have shown an interest in computers and technology, being inspired by my grandfather always having the newest stuff

Later on, I have developed a true dedication towards the IT sector, I did some self learning. that way I got multiple certificates which you can see on my homepage.
